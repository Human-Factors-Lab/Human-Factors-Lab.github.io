On **August 30, 2017**, Dr. Ziho Kang received the **Best Presentation Award** at the IEEE BioSmart Conference in Paris, France.

The recognized presentation focused on a **real-time eye movement analysis framework**, a topic that aligns closely with the lab's broader work in human performance analysis, eye tracking, and dynamic-task support.

This milestone also marked growing visibility for the lab's work at the intersection of human factors, data analytics, and computational modeling.
