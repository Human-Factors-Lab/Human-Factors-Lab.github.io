On **September 26, 2019**, a new FAA Aviation Research and Development project on the **gamification of visual scanning strategies** began.

The project extends the lab's long-running interest in visual scanning behavior by asking how engaging and game-based interfaces can help trainees learn expert search strategies more effectively.

It connects naturally with the lab's work in eye tracking, training intervention, and human-centered system design.
