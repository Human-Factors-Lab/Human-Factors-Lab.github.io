In **July 2021**, the lab published the paper **"Visual search and conflict mitigation strategies used by expert En Route air traffic controllers"** in *Aerospace*.

The paper highlights how expert controllers search complex radar displays and manage conflict mitigation in dynamic environments, continuing the lab's broader FAA-related work in eye tracking, behavior characterization, and training design.

The publication reflects an important research thread connecting air traffic control expertise with quantitative analysis methods.
